# Source Provenance

Source provenance makes every synthesized page auditable. Aperture shows where an idea came from, how strongly each source contributed, and when the page changed.

## What Aperture tracks

- Source links from body sections, frontmatter, absorb logs, and contribution metadata.
- Contribution levels: high, medium, low, or unknown.
- Evolution events such as created, absorbed, merged, split, refined, and linked.

## Why agents need it

Agents can answer from the wiki without pretending every page is equally verified. Provenance gives them a surface for caveats, follow-up reading, and human review.
