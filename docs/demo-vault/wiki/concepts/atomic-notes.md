# Atomic Notes

Atomic notes keep one idea per page so humans and agents can move knowledge without dragging unrelated context along for the ride.

## Why it matters

An atomic page is small enough to verify, link, reuse, and rewrite. When a note grows into multiple claims, Aperture routes it into [[concepts/maps-of-content|Maps of Content]] or splits it into related concept pages.

## Operating rules

- Capture one durable idea, not a source summary.
- Keep evidence visible through the Sources panel.
- Link up to parent MOCs and sideways to sibling concepts.
- Leave judgment status explicit until a human confirms it.
