# Aperture Launch Video Design

## Visual Identity

Warm editorial product demo: parchment background, ink typography, restrained orange accent, graph-line motifs, and real UI screenshots framed like printed evidence.

## Colors

- Paper: `#faf7f1`
- Ink: `#201913`
- Muted: `#5f5145`
- Line: `#e4d9ca`
- Accent: `#a94315`
- Graph green: `#557d65`
- Graph blue: `#5d7f98`

## Typography

- Headlines: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif
- Body: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif

## Motion

- Gentle editorial reveals.
- Slow screenshot push-in.
- Thin graph lines draw in before nodes appear.
- No generic neon, no dark tech gradients, no stock imagery.
